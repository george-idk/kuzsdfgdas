Project Proposal / Bolton Town Centre

Community Canvas

A collaborative public art project inviting Bolton residents, artists, young people and community groups to help create a shared painted space in the heart of the town centre.

View Proposal

Concept

Community Canvas would transform a selected public space into a colourful, community-made artwork, using paint, pattern and local identity.

Aims

  • Brighten Bolton town centre
  • Support local creativity
  • Encourage public participation
  • Create a shared civic artwork

Location

The project would take place on a suitable pavement, square or public surface in Bolton town centre, subject to approval and safety checks.

Delivery

The canvas could be prepared in advance, with residents invited to add designs during a supervised public painting day or weekend event.

Safety

Permission, non-slip materials, supervision, accessibility and a full risk assessment would be required before installation.

Impact

The project would make the town centre feel more welcoming, creative and community-owned.
