Community Canvas

A modern collaborative painting project designed to turn a selected town centre surface into a shared public artwork created by residents, artists, schools and community groups.

Proposal

Community Canvas Bolton would create a supervised public painting space in Bolton town centre, giving local people a direct role in shaping a colourful and welcoming civic artwork.

01

Vision

To create a bold, accessible and community-owned artwork that celebrates Bolton’s identity.

02

Method

A prepared surface would be divided into sections, with participants adding artwork under guidance.

03

Materials

Paints and coatings would be selected for safety, durability, accessibility and easy maintenance.

04

Management

Volunteers, organisers and artists would supervise the site throughout the painting period.

The project could be delivered as a one-day event, a weekend activity or a longer community programme depending on permissions, funding and site suitability.

Location

Bolton Town Centre

Site Criteria

High visibility, safe pedestrian access, enough space for participation and minimal obstruction.

Public Access

The site should remain accessible and should not block key routes, crossings, entrances or emergency access.

Approval

The final location would require approval from the council, landowner and any relevant highways authority.

Impact

The project is designed to make the town centre feel more creative, participatory and welcoming.
